FAQ

VR (Virtual Reality), is a new simulation technology that gained popularity in the last decade. The simulated world can be similar to real world or can de completely different world with different rules. The most import thing is how the user experience that simulated reality.

Board games are the games that can be played on a surface that involve dices or pieces a prepared surface or "board", according to a set of rules. They can be played with people range from 2 to 10 in general. They are perfect way to have fun, socialize and play together.

Sometimes people cannot play board games because they do not have a game or a person to play with. We aim to connect distant people with the games in our library and let them have fun together with the most realistic way.

We used mainly latest version of Unity, Oculus SDK for VR integration and Photon framework for multiplayer support. We also consumed lots of coffee and music during the development.

Now we only support Oculus Quest, but we are trying to expand our options.
